About Us

We are a group of young people with common interests. Led by our personal experience in cooking througout our student lives, we decided to create this website. Here, we've gathered all delicious and affordable recepies, creating a vibrant free online cookbook for every kind of taste. Our main motive is to change the mainstream expression: pasta is the student's dish!
